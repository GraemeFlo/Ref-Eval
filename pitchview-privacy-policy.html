1 Information We Collect

PitchView is designed as a privacy-first tool. The App collects only the information you actively enter during an evaluation session. This may include:

  • Match information (team names, competition, date, venue, score)
  • Referee and assistant referee names and certification levels
  • Your name and evaluator ID as the assessor
  • Match events you log (fouls, cards, goals, offsides, penalties)
  • Critical Match Incident (CMI) details
  • Post-match ratings and assessment notes
  • Evaluator observations and feedback text
Important: PitchView does not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any device sensor data. The App does not require account registration and does not track your behavior across sessions.

2 How We Use Your Information

Information you enter into PitchView is used solely to:

  • Display match data and statistics during live match tracking
  • Generate post-match evaluation reports (PDF and print)
  • Power the AI Feedback feature (see Section 3)
  • Save your theme preference locally on your device

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3 AI-Powered Features & Anthropic API

PitchView's AI Feedback feature sends match data to the Anthropic API to generate coaching-focused evaluation narratives. When you tap "Generate AI Feedback," the following information is transmitted to Anthropic's servers:

  • Match details (teams, competition, date, score, statistics)
  • The match event log
  • Official names and roles
  • Ratings and assessment notes you have entered
  • Critical Match Incidents you have logged
  • Any evaluator notes you have typed
Please be aware: Data sent to the Anthropic API is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Do not include sensitive personal information (such as player medical details or personal addresses) in any notes or descriptions within the App.

The AI Feedback feature requires an active internet connection. All other features of PitchView work fully offline once the App is loaded.

4 Data Storage

PitchView stores data as follows:

  • Session data (match log, scores, events, ratings) — stored in your browser's memory during the active session only. This data is not persisted between sessions unless you export it as a PDF.
  • Theme preference — saved to your device's local storage (localStorage). This stays on your device and is never transmitted.
  • PDF exports — saved to your device's local storage (Downloads or Files). These remain under your control.
No server-side storage: PitchView does not operate a database or backend server. Your evaluation data is never stored on our servers.

5 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Anthropic — when you use the AI Feedback feature (see Section 3)
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or legal process

PDF reports you generate and share manually are your responsibility. Please handle referee evaluation reports in accordance with your organization's confidentiality policies.

6 Children's Privacy

PitchView is intended for use by adult referee evaluators and assessors. The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. While match data may reference youth soccer games, no personal information about players or minors is collected or required by the App.

7 Your Rights

Because PitchView does not store personal data on a server, most traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are exercised simply by:

  • Closing the browser session to clear all match data
  • Clearing your browser's localStorage to remove theme preferences
  • Deleting any PDF reports you have saved to your device

For data transmitted to Anthropic via the AI Feedback feature, please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for your rights regarding that data.

8 Security

PitchView is served over HTTPS when hosted on GitHub Pages, ensuring data in transit is encrypted. The App does not store credentials, payment information, or sensitive personal data. API communications with Anthropic are made over encrypted HTTPS connections.

As with any web application, you should ensure you are using a trusted, secure device when conducting evaluations containing sensitive information.
